FH-Koeln Erasmus Guide

Welcome to our Erasmus Students Guide Website

So you've heard that life in Germany as an Erasmus Student is kind of a big deal, and you're not sure how to adobt all the changes and get involved in the international student atmosphere here in cologne
Don't Worry we will guide you to all what you need to become a succesful part of our community.

This website is designed to teach you what you need to know become a successful member and an active part in the international exchange student time here in cologne.

here is a list of things you will need in order to enroll in the university and daily life:

  • find a living place
  • register your address with the government
  • get yourself an insurance
  • transfer semester fee to the university
  • obtain your VRS & NRW tickets
  • check courses and attend the ones you would like to take


find a living place

It is very hard to find an accomodation here in cologne, thus we recommend that you start as early as you can, searching for an accomodation will require quiet a good time and mostly done online (don't worry, its safe). here you can find some of the options that would help you to find an accomodation:

  • Koelner StudentenWerk
  • www.wg-gesucht.de
  • exampleWebsite.com
  • or a company that would do that job for you

register your address with the government

This is pretty easily done, all you have to do is to search online for the closest "Rathaus" to you and go there give them your new address and don't forget to take your passport with you.

get yourself an insurance

now, getting yourself an insurance can be quiet tricky, but in order to be able to successfuly enroll in the university you need it, hoping that you will always be healthy we suggest that you go to the nearest insurance company to you to to get your insurance done.

there are several insurance companies around yet the most known are:

  • AOK
  • TK

transfer semester fee to the university

Once you finish all of the above, you take all your information and give it to our great collegues at our International Office, they will register you and get you on track, within a week you will recieve a letter by the post that will hold your Student-id as well as the payment information with the instructions, follow the instructions in order for the enrollment process to go as smooth as planned.

obtain your VRS & NRW tickets

VRS Ticket

Once you transfer the money and your semester stars, you can go to one of the machines located by our MENSA "cafeteria" and insert your card, the machine will print the VRS ticket on your card and you will be able to use the VRS at 0 cost.

NRW Ticket

for the NRW ticket all you need to do is to log in into your student account and print it out on a paper, your student account and password will be sent to you by mail by our university after your enrollment.

courses and attend the ones you would like to take

before the semester starts, a meeting for all erasmus students will be held. in this meeting you will get to know the people responsible for your stay, studies as well as other students.

during this meeting we will help you figure out the courses that you can attend, which leaves the choice to you for what courses to take and attend, after attending the courses you will get an idea about them and then all that is left for you is to sign up for the examination and pass. congrats by now you have all the general infomation that you need to get stuff done.

for more detailed information, please refer to the other pages where we will describe in detail some processes of the mentioned above
